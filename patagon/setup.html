Why do we need Authority, W9, and Certificate of Insurance?

To ensure that we can provide you with the best possible service, we require certain documents from our clients. These documents include:

Authority: This document is issued by the Federal Motor Carrier Safety Administration (FMCSA) and is required for all motor carriers operating in the United States. The Authority gives the carrier the right to transport freight, and it ensures that the carrier meets all safety and insurance requirements.

W9: The W9 is a tax form that provides us with your company's tax identification number (TIN) for tax reporting purposes.

Certificate of Insurance: This document provides proof of insurance coverage for the carrier. It ensures that the carrier has the necessary insurance coverage to protect your shipment in case of loss or damage.

By providing us with these documents, you can be assured that we are working with carriers who meet all necessary safety and insurance requirements. This, in turn, ensures that your shipment is in safe hands and arrives at its destination on time and in excellent condition.
